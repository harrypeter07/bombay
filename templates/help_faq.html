Help & FAQ

Help Topics

How to register for an account?

1. Go to the login page.

2. Click on the 'Register' link.

3. Fill out the registration form and submit.

How to reset your password?

1. Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page.

2. Enter your registered email address.

3. Follow the instructions in the password reset email.

How to create a segmentation?

1. Go to the 'Create Segmentation' page.

2. Enter your prompt in the input box.

3. Click 'Generate' to create the segmentation.

Understanding the dashboard overview.

1. The dashboard provides an overview of your application's key features.

2. Use the navigation menu to access different sections.

3. Check the 'Latest Updates' section for recent changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an account?

You can register by clicking the 'Register' link on the login page and filling in the required details.

What if I forget my password?

Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

How do I create a segmentation?

Navigate to the 'Create Segmentation' page from the dashboard, enter your prompt, and click 'Generate' to create the segmentation.

How do I view the generated JSON?

After creating a segmentation, you can view the generated JSON on the 'View JSON' page accessible from the dashboard.
